Energie Passagen was an installation in front of the Literaturhaus MÃ¼nchen. It reflected the energy of the city by projecting the most relevant words from RSS feeds of the newspaper. Visitors were able to explore the information river through a voice recognition system. By selecting words of interest people could rebuild their own version of current events.

Programming Language: C++/OpenGL, Java, Flash, MaxMSP
